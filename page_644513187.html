Aims of the project

Nov. 17, 2010

Goals

 The purpose of this project is for you to gain

  1. Deeper knowledge about the application of Organic Compounds(Organic Chemistry)
  2. Skills in online research and information processing (Literacy)
  3. Basic Principles on developing Good Website (ICT) 

Each group must create a website that shows your understanding on the application of Organic Compounds.  The website should include

  • Description of a specific organic compound
  • Description of the ways in which the organic compound is used in everyday household items
  • Description of the social factors that play part in the use of the organic compound in household items
  • Suggestion on substitutes, regulation or monitoring of the organic compound to ascertain product safety (choose one)
