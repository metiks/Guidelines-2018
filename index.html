The Everyday Household - Guidelines

About

This project addresses the application of organic compounds in household items. It is intended to allow grade 11 students to build their knowledge on organic compounds, its uses in household items and the relationship to human health. The students will work collaboratively in groups and communicate with other students to express their knowledge and ideas by building an e-book & web pages.

CURRICULUM STANDARDS:
• Understanding organic compounds and its uses in household items (Organic Chemistry)
• Using digital media and environments to communicate and work
collaboratively to support individual learning and contribute to the learning of others (ICT)

EXPECTED OUTCOME

Students will have the abilities to
• Identify different types of organic compounds currently in
available in everyday household items
• Describe the impact of these household items (safe & dangerous) on
people’s health
• Describe different ways to influence and ascertain safe use
of organic compounds
• Design a website to communicate and express their knowledge
and ideas

The project's essential problem, question or issue:
How safe are the household items that we use and what can we contribute to make our lives safer?
